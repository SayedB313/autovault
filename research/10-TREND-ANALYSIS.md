## Technology Trends Driving Opportunity

1. **IoT Sensor Integration** — Climate monitoring, humidity, temperature, and security sensors are becoming affordable and reliable enough for facility-wide deployment
2. **Cloud Infrastructure** — Real-time dashboards, mobile notifications, and multi-facility management enabled by mature cloud platforms
3. **Mobile-First Experiences** — Luxury consumers expect app-level experiences for managing high-value assets
4. **API Ecosystem** — Insurance providers, detailers, transport companies, and auction houses are increasingly API-enabled, enabling marketplace integrations

---

## Consumer Behavior Trends

1. **Status-Driven Ownership** — Luxury vehicles increasingly treated as investment assets requiring professional management
2. **Provenance Premium** — Documented vehicle history (climate logs, service records) directly impacts resale value at auction
3. **Concierge Expectations** — HNW individuals expect white-glove digital experiences matching their lifestyle across all service categories
4. **Seasonal Storage Cycles** — Predictable demand patterns around winter storage, event prep, and estate management

---

## Competitive Landscape Trend

- **No purpose-built software** exists for luxury car storage operations
- **Storeganise** is the closest competitor but lacks vehicle-specific features
- **Manual operations** (spreadsheets, texts, phone calls) are the dominant "competitor"
- **Blue ocean** opportunity with first-mover advantage available
