# PROVA - Market Gap Analysis

**Overall Rating: 8/10**

The biggest market gap exists in digitizing the luxury car storage niche with a streamlined client portal. As luxury car ownership grows, existing manual methods are inefficient and error-prone, highlighting a clear need for digital solutions.

## Underserved Customer Segments (8/10)

### Luxury Car Owners
Luxury car owners demand real-time service visibility and vehicle status updates, which current manual systems cannot provide. [2]
