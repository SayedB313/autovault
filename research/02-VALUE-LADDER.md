# PROVA - Value Ladder & Offer Strategy

---

## Offer Tiers

### 1. LEAD MAGNET (Bait)
**Luxury Car Management Demo** - FREE

An interactive web app demo showcasing real-time updates and scheduling for luxury car storage. Lets facility operators see what the dashboard looks like with sample vehicles, climate data, and scheduling workflows.

**Purpose:** Get operators hands-on with the product. Reduce friction to first touch.

---

### 2. FRONTEND
**Founder's Edition Access** - $99/month (first three months)

Early access to the platform for limited users, including premium support and feedback sessions. This is a discounted onboarding tier designed to lock in early adopters who will shape the product.

**Purpose:** Convert demo users into paying customers. Build case studies and testimonials.

---

### 3. CORE OFFER
**Premium Storage Management Subscription** - $500-$1,500/month

Comprehensive service for managing luxury car storage with features like real-time vehicle status updates and maintenance scheduling. Price scales with facility size (number of vehicles stored).

**Purpose:** Primary revenue driver. Sticky subscription with near-zero churn.

---

### 4. BACKEND (Future)
**Marketplace Commission** - 10-20% per service job

Every service request (detailing, maintenance, transport) runs through a vetted marketplace of providers. Prova takes a cut on each transaction.

**Purpose:** Revenue multiplier that grows with facility volume.

---

### 5. CONTINUITY (Future)
**Provenance Data Access** - TBD pricing

Vehicle histories, climate logs, and service records accumulate over years. That documented provenance follows the car through its life. Owners and auction houses pay to access verified vehicle history data.

**Purpose:** Long-term data monetization. Creates an entirely new revenue stream from accumulated operational data.

---

## Pricing Strategy Notes

- **$500-$1,500/month** scales by facility size (number of bays/vehicles)
- **Near-zero churn** once operational data is embedded in the system
- **Marketplace take rate** (10-20%) adds revenue without adding subscription cost
- **Provenance data** is a future moat - years of accumulated data becomes increasingly valuable
- Sales cycle is slow but the pitch is simple: the cars are worth more than most real estate portfolios
