## PART 1: BUSINESS CLASSIFICATION

### Business Model & Market
- **Business Type:** B2B
- **Market Position:** Storeganise lacks vehicle-specific features. Manual systems dominate, but they are inefficient and error-prone.

### Target Audience

**Buyer Personas:**
- Luxury car owners
- High-end car storage businesses
- Automotive event planners

**Key Pain Points:**
- Disorganized communication
- Lack of real-time service visibility
- Manual tracking inefficiencies

---

## PART 2: PHASE 1 ROADMAP (0-6 MONTHS)

### Core Strategy

**MVP Approach:** Develop a basic portal integrating vehicle status and service scheduling.

**Initial Offer:**
| Field              | Value                                    |
|--------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Model              | Subscription                             |
| Price              | $25-$200 per car/month                   |
| Value Proposition  | Real-time updates and service coordination |
| Fulfillment        | Online portal access                     |

### Lead Generation Strategy

**Lead Magnet:**
- Type & Format: Web App Demo - Interactive portal tour
- Expected Conversion: 10-15%
- Value Demonstration: Showcase real-time updates and scheduling ease

**Acquisition Channels:**
- Facebook Groups (Weekly campaigns)
- Active luxury car communities
- Content Format: Educational content, user stories
- Target Metrics: 200 signups/month
