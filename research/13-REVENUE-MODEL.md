# PROVA - Revenue Model & Financial Projections

---

## Revenue Potential: $1M-$10M ARR

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. SaaS Subscription (Primary)
- **Price:** $500-$1,500/month per facility
- **Scales by:** Number of vehicles / facility size
- **Churn:** Near zero once operational data accumulates
- **Why sticky:** Vehicle histories, climate logs, and service records build over years

### 2. Marketplace Commission (Secondary)
- **Take Rate:** 10-20% per service job
- **Services:** Detailing, maintenance, transport, battery tending
- **Average job value:** $200-$2,000+
- **Revenue per job:** $20-$400
- **Volume driver:** More facilities = more service requests

### 3. Enhanced Service Add-Ons (Tertiary)
- **Price:** $100-$500/feature/month
- **Examples:** Advanced climate analytics, transport coordination, insurance integration
- **Upsell mechanism:** Start with core, add modules as needs grow

### 4. Backend Offer (Future)
- **Price:** $10,000+/year
- **Product:** Ultimate Luxury Management Package (car + yacht + jet management)
- **Target:** Ultra-HNW individuals with diversified luxury assets

### 5. Provenance Data (Future)
- **Price:** TBD (per-report or subscription)
- **Buyers:** Auction houses, insurance companies, buyers in private sales
- **Value:** Verified climate-controlled storage history adds resale value

## Pricing Strategy Notes

- **Founder's Edition:** $99/month for first 3 months (loss leader for case studies)
- **Per-car pricing option:** $25-$200 per car/month (alternative to flat facility fee)
- **Sales cycle:** Slow (enterprise B2B) but high-conviction once demo'd
- **Key pitch:** "The cars in your building are worth more than most real estate portfolios — your management software should reflect that."
