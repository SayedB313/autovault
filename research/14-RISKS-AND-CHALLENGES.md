# PROVA - Risks & Challenges

---

## Technical Risks

### IoT Integration Challenges (Medium Risk)
- **Risk:** Integrating with various climate sensor hardware across different facilities
- **Mitigation:** Start with 2-3 popular sensor brands, build adapter layer for extensibility
- **Score:** 6/10

### Data Security & Privacy (High Risk)
- **Risk:** Storing sensitive vehicle and owner data (VINs, insurance, contact info)
- **Mitigation:** SOC 2 compliance, encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access
- **Score:** 8/10 importance

---

## Market Risks (Low — 2/10)

### Slow Sales Cycle
- **Risk:** Enterprise B2B sales to facility operators take time
- **Mitigation:** Start with warm intros, offer free pilot periods, build case studies fast

### Market Size Ceiling
- **Risk:** Total addressable market for luxury car storage facilities may be limited
- **Mitigation:** Expand to yacht and private jet management (Phase 2 strategy)

### Competitor Entry
- **Risk:** Storeganise or a new entrant adds vehicle-specific features
- **Mitigation:** Move fast, accumulate data moat, build marketplace network effects

---

## Operational Risks (Low — 2/10)

### Resistance from Traditional Operators
- **Risk:** Facility operators comfortable with manual processes may resist adoption
- **Mitigation:** Provide training materials, white-glove onboarding, show immediate time savings

### Marketplace Cold Start
- **Risk:** Not enough service providers initially to create a good marketplace experience
- **Mitigation:** Build marketplace last (Phase 3), only after sufficient facility volume

---

## Financial Risks

### Capital Requirements
- **Risk:** Tagged as "Capital Intensive" — may require meaningful investment to scale
- **Mitigation:** MVP is $0-10K budget; revenue from early facilities funds growth
- **Bootstrap-friendly:** Solo founder with AI tools can build MVP

### Long Payback on Sales Effort
- **Risk:** Slow sales cycle means upfront effort before revenue materializes
- **Mitigation:** Target facilities already in pain (manual errors, lost clients), convert fastest
