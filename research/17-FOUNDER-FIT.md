# PROVA - Founder Fit Assessment

---

## Quick Assessment

### Best For
- Founders with experience in luxury automotive services
- Teams adept in high-end client relationship management

### Less Ideal For
- Founders lacking luxury market experience
- Non-technical founders without integration skills

### Assessment Note
Evaluated on luxury market experience and tech integration ability.

---

## Ideal Founder Profile

### Must-Have Skills
1. **Technical capability** — Can build the MVP (web app, database, API integrations)
2. **Luxury market understanding** — Knows HNW customer expectations
3. **B2B sales ability** — Can run in-person demos and close enterprise deals
4. **IoT/hardware familiarity** — Comfortable integrating with physical sensors

### Nice-to-Have
1. Existing network in the luxury car community (clubs, events, facilities)
2. Experience with marketplace businesses (two-sided platforms)
3. Understanding of vehicle provenance and auction markets
4. Previous startup experience in vertical SaaS

### Deal-Breakers
1. No technical ability and no technical co-founder
2. No patience for slow B2B sales cycles (this isn't a viral consumer app)
3. No access to or interest in the luxury automotive world

---

## Self-Assessment Questions

1. Can you name 5 luxury car storage facilities? Do you know anyone who operates one?
2. Have you ever attended a concours d'elegance, SEMA, or similar automotive event?
3. Can you build a web application with database + API integrations?
4. Are you comfortable with 30-90 day enterprise sales cycles?
5. Do you have 6-12 months of runway to build before reaching meaningful revenue?

**If you answered "no" to 3+ of these, this idea may not be the best fit — unless you have a co-founder who covers the gaps.**
